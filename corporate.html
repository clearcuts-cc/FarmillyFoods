Policy

Refund Policy

Simple and transparent support for fresh, perishable products.

Farmmily Farms and Foods does not offer refunds. Because our products are fresh and perishable, confirmed orders are eligible only for a replacement where support review confirms a valid issue.

If your order arrives damaged, spoiled, missing, or materially incorrect, contact us as soon as possible with your order ID, photos, and a short description of the issue.

After review, our team may approve a replacement for the affected item or order. Replacement decisions are made case by case to protect both product quality and customer fairness.

Issues reported too late after delivery, or without enough supporting details, may not qualify for replacement.

For help, contact support@farmmilyfoods.com or WhatsApp +91 73589 52051.

Policy

Shipping Policy

Fresh dispatch timelines for seasonal orders and pantry products.

Orders are typically delivered within 3 to 10 days depending on product type, harvest timing, destination, and serviceability.

Fresh mango and seasonal harvest orders may be dispatched according to ripeness and packing readiness so your delivery reaches you in the best possible condition.

Delivery timelines may vary during high-demand periods, weather disruption, remote-area routing, or public holidays.

Once your order is confirmed, you can track the current status from the Track Order page using your order ID or registered phone number.

For urgent shipping help, contact support@farmmilyfoods.com or WhatsApp +91 73589 52051.
